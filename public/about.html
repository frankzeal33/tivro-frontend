About Us

WHAT WE DO

Innovating Rental Experiences Across Africa

Tivro Africa was built with one mission: to make the rental process faster, more secure, and more transparent for everyone in Africa. We provide tech-powered solutions for verifying tenants and properties, reducing risks, and ensuring smooth transactions.

Whether you're renting a property or looking for a place to live, Tivro's verification platform is built for you.

Our Journey

Started with a vision to simplify the rental process across Africa, Tivro Africa has quickly become the go-to platform for secure, fast, and transparent tenant and property verification.

WHAT WE DO

Our Evolving Journey

Tivro was born out of a necessity to solve a critical problem in the real estate industry, the challenge of verifying the credibility of potential tenants. The idea was conceived by a team of industry experts who recognised the significant risks property owners face when accepting tenants without thorough background checks. With years of experience in property management, finance, and technology, our founders set out to create a solution that would revolutionise tenant verification.

From our humble beginnings, we have grown into a comprehensive platform that is trusted by thousands of property owners and real estate professionals. Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, driven by our commitment to excellence and our desire to make the tenant screening process as straightforward and effective as possible.
